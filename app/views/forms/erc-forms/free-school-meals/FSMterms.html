Clothing Grants and Free School Meals

Terms and Conditions

This form is for applying for clothing grants and free school meals for school year 2020/21

  • Your child(ren) must attend a school within East Renfrewshire
  • Clothing grants and free school meals are not available to children attending nursery school
  • P1, P2 and P3 children will all receive free school meals - you do not need to apply for these
  • If your child is age 16 or over, or starting P1 or S1, has moved school or if you have made a placing request, their clothing grant will be paid once confirmation of their attendance at school has been received
  • The closing date for all applications is 31st May 2021
  • Once we have received your application and verified your entitlement we aim to process your application within 15 working days
