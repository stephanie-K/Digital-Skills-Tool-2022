What if my postcode is right but my address can't be found?

If you live in a new development, a newly built property, or if the Royal Mail have recently updated your post code, we may not be able to find your address.

If this is the case, you can attempt to manually enter your address by leaving the house name/number blank, and only entering your post code; you can then enter each line of your address manually.

If your post code is not recognised, you will not be able to continue with your online application at the present time. You will need to complete form D1 'Application for a driving licence', available from the DVLA form ordering service or most Post Offices.
