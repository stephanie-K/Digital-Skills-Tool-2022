What is a National Insurance number?

Every National Insurance number is different. It can consist of either 8 or 9 characters and is made up of letters and numbers, in the format of AANNNNNN(A) where A is alphabetic and N is numeric, the final character is not always available. Please see the examples below:

  • FD274136A or FD274136

(Please note that this National Insurance number is just an example and should not be used as your own number.)

Your National Insurance number is your own personal number. Only you can use it.
