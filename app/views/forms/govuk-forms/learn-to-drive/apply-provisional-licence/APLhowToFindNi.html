How to find your National Insurance number

If you think you already have a number but can't remember it, you might be able to find it on official paperwork you've got at home, for instance:

  • National Insurance card
  • Your end of year statement of tax (P60)
  • A payslip
  • A copy of your annual tax return
  • Other official correspondence

Your National Insurance number will not change, even if you go abroad, marry, register as a civil partner, change your name, etc.

If you still can't find your number, contact your local Jobcentre Plus, social security office or HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) NI Contributions Office and they'll tell you what to do. Contact details for these organisations can be found at www.dvla.gov.uk
