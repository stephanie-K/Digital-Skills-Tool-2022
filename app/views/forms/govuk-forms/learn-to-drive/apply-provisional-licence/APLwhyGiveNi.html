Why should you provide your National Insurance number?

Providing your National Insurance number can:

  • Improve the chance of completing your application online, saving you time
  • Where appropriate, avoid having to send in additional paper documentation such as original ID documents by post
  • Your National Insurance number will not be retained by the DVLA once you have submitted your application
