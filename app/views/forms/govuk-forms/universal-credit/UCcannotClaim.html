You cannot claim Universal Credit at the moment

You cannot claim Universal Credit if you get or are entitled to the severe disability premium.

What to do next

You can keep claiming other benefits because you get or are entitled to the severe disability premium.

Call one of the numbers below to make or continue a claim. When you call, tell them that you get or are entitled to the severe disability premium.

Make or continue a claim

Call 0800 169 0350 to make or continue a claim for:

  • Income Support
  • Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)
  • Jobseeker's Allowance (JSA)

To make or continue a Housing Benefit claim, contact your local authority.

For a Tax Credits claim, call 0345 300 3900 (textphone: 0345 300 3909).

If you live in Northern Ireland

To make or continue a claim in Northern Ireland, call:

  • 0800 022 4250 for Income Support or Jobseeker's Allowance
  • 0800 587 1377 (textphone: 0800 328 3419) for Employment and Support Allowance
  • 0345 300 3900 (textphone: 0345 300 3909) for tax credits

Contact the Northern Ireland Housing Executive to make or continue a Housing Benefit claim.
