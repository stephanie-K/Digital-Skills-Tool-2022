Income other than earnings

You need to tell us about any money that's not from work.

Do not include:

  • child maintenance payments
  • State Pension
  • UK benefits - such as Child Benefit or Disability Living Allowance

Do include:

  • personal maintenance payments
  • personal or occupational pensions
  • mortgage payment protection
  • insurance payments
  • annuity payments
  • student finance
